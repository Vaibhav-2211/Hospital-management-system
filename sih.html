Medical Emergency Procedures

In case of an emergency, follow these steps:

  • Call for emergency services immediately.
  • Check if the patient is conscious and breathing.
  • Perform CPR if necessary and trained to do so.
  • Administer basic first aid until medical help arrives.
  • Provide necessary information to the paramedics.
